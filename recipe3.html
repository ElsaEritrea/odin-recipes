Margherita Pizza

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 200g mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Olive oil
  • Salt

Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 220°C (425°F).

2. Roll out the pizza dough and place it on a pizza stone or baking sheet.

3. Spread the tomato sauce evenly over the dough.

4. Add slices of mozzarella cheese and drizzle with olive oil.

5. Bake for 10-12 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted.

6. Top with fresh basil leaves and a pinch of salt before serving.
