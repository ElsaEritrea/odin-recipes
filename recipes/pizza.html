Pizza

Description

This pizza recipe gives you a perfectly crispy crust, savory sauce, melted cheese, and your favorite toppings.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Pepperoni
  • Mushrooms

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the dough and spread the tomato sauce.
  3. Add cheese and your favorite toppings.
  4. Bake for 10-12 minutes or until golden and bubbly.
  5. Let cool for a few minutes, then slice and serve.
