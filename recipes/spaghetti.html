Spaghetti

Description

This spaghetti recipe is a classic Italian dish with rich tomato sauce and perfectly cooked noodles.

Ingredients

  • 12 ounces spaghetti noodles
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Boil the spaghetti noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Saut√© garlic in olive oil. Add marinara sauce and simmer for 5 minutes.
  3. Toss the spaghetti in the sauce and serve with Parmesan cheese on top.
